about me

My name is Judd Gatbonton. I am a recent graduate from UC San Diego with a strong passion for Human Computer Interaction and Computer Science.

Through my studies, I have gained knowledge in both design and development. I have a great desire to always create what's next and put the user experience first. I believe in the importantance of thoughtfulness for the users I am designing for.

I love building software applications that are both aesthetically pleasing and easy to use. My interests lie in the user experience, interaction design, and front end development.

In my spare time, I enjoy powerlifting, exploring, and Star Wars.
